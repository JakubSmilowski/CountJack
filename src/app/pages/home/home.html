Essential Basic Strategy Rules

📋
HIT 👆
  • Always hit on 12–16 vs dealer 7–Ace
  • Hit soft 17 (A,6) vs dealer 9,10,A
  • Hit any total of 11 or less
STAND ✋
  • Always stand on hard 17 or more
  • Stand on 12–16 vs dealer 2–6
  • Stand on soft 19–20 (A,8–A,9)
DOUBLE ⬆️
  • Double 11 vs dealer 2–10
  • Double 10 vs dealer 2–9
  • Double 9 vs dealer 3–6
SPLIT ↔️
  • Always split Aces and 8s
  • Never split 10s, 5s, or 4s
  • Split 9s vs dealer 2–9 (except 7)

Card Counting Basics

🔢

Card counting gives you an edge by tracking the ratio of high to low cards remaining in the deck. While not illegal, it requires practice and discipline.

⚠️
Legal Notice: Card counting is a mental skill and is legal in most jurisdictions. However, casinos may ask you to leave if they suspect you're counting cards. Always play responsibly and within your means.
